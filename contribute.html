Do you have a personal story and experience about the impact of climate change?
Do you have a solution?
Then contribute to the archive of the Climate Wunderkammer!
The first call closed by the end of April 2023 and collected the first set of projects that you can see exhibited in the Collective Archive at the inauguration of the Biennale in May 2023.

Starting from May 18th 2023, during the inauguration of the Climate Wunderkammer project, the call opens again, aiming at incrementing the growth of the digital archive, thanks to the high visibility given by the Biennale.

Take part! Are you a designer or a planner or working in the creative industry? Are you a professional or a student in the field? Then you are eligible for submitting your narrative or your solution now. Selected projects will be included in the archive and will be exhibited during the Biennale until November 2023.
